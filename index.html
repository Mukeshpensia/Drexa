ONLINE CODING COMMUNITY FOR KIDS WHO WANTS TO CODE

what we do

Drexa World is a community for kids who want to code. Our goal is to make learning to code more fun and efficient for K12 students. We built this platform to revolutionize the way programming is being taught to kids. Drexa World provides live one on one coding for kids. We have a team of instructors who hold strong computer science backgrounds and an innovator mindset. We believe that every kid is an innovator and with the right support they can do great things. We use various coding games & pre recorded videos to make the whole learning process more engaging and fun. We focus on individualized learning so that we can help every kid learn faster. Each Kid has their own learning journey, for instance, coding for teens is more challenging than for 8 years old beginners.

key features

Develop Skills

Our one on one live teaching strategy focuses on supporting each kid individually and helps them in developing problem-solving & critical thinking skills.

Learning Videos

We have a collection of self-learning videos on our platform using which kids can get an in-depth understanding of not just coding but also of core computer science fundamentals.

Free Games

Also we provide free games on the platform through which Kids can learn coding in an engaging environment that utilizes gaming elements.

Overall Development

Our curriculum is designed in a way that it not only helps kids in learning coding, but in overalldevelopment of them. We focus on improving logical thinking and problem solving skills of kids using coding.

why drexa world ?

Drexa World is a community for kids who want to code. Our fun based learning model helps in making learning to code more engaging and less stressful. We provide one on one live teaching on our platform that offers a variety of computer programming courses. Also we have a collection of pre-recorded self-learning fun videos, and games that bridge the gap between wanting to code and becoming an expert in the field. The courses are user-friendly and intuitive so children can jump right into them without needing an introductory course or previous coding background. Drexa World teachers are from top-tier universities and have a great understanding of computer science. Our curriculum is designed by computer science professionals from Ivy League institutes. It covers a wide range of topics in computer science and gives proper training to kids in becoming computer scientists of tomorrow. We focus on making learning easier, faster and more fun.
